My Top Five Concerts

In my time working for the newspaper, I was able to do some freelance writing and photography and it came with its perks! I was able to see a lot of my favorite artists live and learn of some new ones!

#5 Lila Downs

Lila Downs, her homemade costumes and amazing vocals are well worth any show and movie soundtrack.

#4. Daddy Yankee

I'm not a fan of Reggeaton music but I have to admit that Daddy Yankee put up a great show and is great at pumping up his audience.

#3. Natalia Lafourcade

Natalia Lafourcade is a very talented singer/songwriter, producer and actress. Her music is very pop-like and enjoyable.

#2. De La Soul

De La Soul is one of the best lyrical hip hop bands that's been around since the early 90's. I was fortunate enought to see them live, in part of a former job that consisted of me attending concerts.

#1. Los Amigos Invisibles

I first heard the venezuelan band in 2010 in one of their performances at the Logan Square Auditorium. I fell in love with their music then, what they call "gozadera", we consider funk music. Their lively performances will have you appreciate their whole discography!
