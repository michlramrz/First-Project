Top Three Artists to See Live

#3. Natalia Lafourcade

Natalia Lafourcade is a very talented singer/songwriter, producer and actress. Her music is very pop-like but enjoyable.

#2. De La Soul

De La Soul is one of the most lyrical hip hop bands that's been around since the early 90's. I was fortunate enought to see them live in part of job that consisted of me attending concerts.

#1. Los Amigos Invisibles

I first heard the venezuelan band in 2010 in one of their performances at the Logan Square Auditorium. I fell in love with their music then, what they call "gozadera", we consider funk music. Their lively performances will have you appreciate their whole discography!
